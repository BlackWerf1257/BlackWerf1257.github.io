### Implicit Intent

#### Definition

- Specifying a designated event to launch an existing application provided by Android

#### Example

- Make a call
  - After passing the phone number via an intent, the relevant app launches

#### Related Methods

- URI: Uniform Resource Identifier
  - In Android, URLs are used as identifiers to access resources and as identification information for execution
  - -Parse() method
